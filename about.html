About Me

Hey, I'm Taiyo — a recent business graduate with a background in finance and a deep interest in design, technology, and systems thinking. I'm drawn to projects that blend creativity and logic — where form meets function and ideas turn into things you can touch or experience.

Over the past few years, I've explored how data, design, and storytelling intersect — from studying markets and building financial models to designing interfaces and experimenting with prototypes. I love figuring out how systems connect and how small details shape the bigger picture.

I'm currently based in Tokyo, finding rhythm between creative work, long-term projects, and personal growth. Whether it's building tools, learning something new, or crafting something meaningful, I'm always looking for ways to make ideas real.

This site is my home base — a space to collect, create, and connect the dots as I keep learning and building.
