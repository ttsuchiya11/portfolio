How to Use

Converting Time

1

Open Timezoner in Messages

In a Messages conversation, tap the Apps button (grid icon), then select Timezoner.

2

Search for a City

Type a city name in the search bar. Tap one of the suggestion bubbles to select it.

3

Adjust the Time

Spin either time picker to find your desired time. Both pickers stay synchronized automatically.

4

Copy and Share

Tap the copy button to copy the time, then paste it into your message. Long-press for format options.

Tip: Long-press the copy button to choose between three formats: just the time, time with "your time" context, or time with the city name.

Managing Saved Cities

1

Switch to Saved Cities Tab

Tap "Saved Cities" at the top of the app to view your saved timezones.

2

Add Cities

Use the search bar to find cities and tap the suggestion bubbles to add them to your list.

3

Organize Your List

Drag cities to reorder them, or swipe left on any city to delete it from your list.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

No Data Collection

Timezoner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely offline on your device.

Local Storage Only

The app stores your preferences (selected timezone and saved cities list) locally on your device using iOS's standard storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not synced to any server
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

No Analytics or Tracking

Timezoner contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. We don't know how you use the app, and we don't want to.

No Network Access

The app makes no network requests. All timezone calculations are performed locally using your device's built-in timezone database.
